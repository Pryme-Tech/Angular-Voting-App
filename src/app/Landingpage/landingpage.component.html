Hosting an Election

  1. Create your Ballot:
    Add Categories such as President,General Secretary e.t.c and candidates of each category to your election.
  2. Add your voters:
    Control who is authorized to vote in your elections. Add voters one by one, or imort them from a spreadsheet.
  3. Launch the Election:
    When you're done customizing the election, you can schedule a start/end date and launch your election. A link will be generated for you to share with your voters.
  4. View Results:
    Watch the results of your election in real-time. At the end of the election you have the option to share your results with your voters.

Voting

  1. Voter clicks on election link:
    The voter will need his/her voting id to access election. The voter then selects one candidate from each category to vote for and then submit the vote.
  2. One man One vote:
    Every voter has a distinct "Voter ID" and is only allowed to cast one ballot.

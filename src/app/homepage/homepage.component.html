A three-option electronic voting process for your voters.
REMOTE VOTING

Remote electronic voting using a computer, mobile device, or tablet permitted under strict conditions that ensure the privacy of the vote and the voter's identification. Remote supervision and assistance.

VOTING STATIONS

Electronic voting in specially designated locations where people can see each other, with trained computers and monitors to guide the process. both in-person and remote supervision and assistance.

HYBRID VOTING

Remote and face-to-face electronic voting, centrally coordinated, with the security measures of both processes. Supervised and assisted remotely and in person.
